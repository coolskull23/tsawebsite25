    Maintain Ronald McDonald House

    Sign Up

    300 E.California Avnue, El Paso, TX 79902, United States

    Join us in helping to clean and maintain the Ronald McDonald House, a home away from home for families with hospitalized children who are facing serious medical challenges. This initiative provides a comfortable and supportive environment for those who cannot afford traditional housing while their loved ones receive necessary medical care. Your efforts will contribute to keeping the house clean, organized, and welcoming, ensuring that families can focus on what truly matters—supporting their children during difficult times.

    Toy Drive Rescue Ministries of Mexico

    Sign Up

    10357 Jon Ct, El Paso, TX 71902, United States

    These toys would go to Rescue Ministries of Mexico, which are orphanages in Mexico. A lot of family’s separate in Mexico trying to cross the border. Kids tend to lose faith, which is seen with the suicide rates peaking between Christmas and New Years. These toys would really help a lot, bringing faith and hope back to the kids. You can drop off at Agape Church on Tuesdays (6:30pm-9:00pm) and Sundays (10:30am-2pm)(11299 Pebble Hills Blvd) or at Americas High School (C201).

    Ronald McDonald House Charities of El Paso

    Sign Up

    300 E.California Avnue, El Paso, TX 79902, United States
